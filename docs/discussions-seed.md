# Discussions Setup and Seed Plan

## Recommended Discussion Categories

1. Announcements
- Maintainer updates, roadmap checkpoints, release highlights.

2. Q&A
- Reader and contributor questions about handbook usage.

3. Ideas
- Proposed chapter additions, structure improvements, new appendices.

4. Show and Tell
- Community examples of how readers used the handbook in practice.

5. Contributor Lounge
- Coordination for open issues and mentorship-style guidance.

## Starter Discussion Posts (Seed Content)

1. Welcome thread: who you are and what Rust goals brought you here
2. Which chapter changed your Rust mental model the most?
3. What chapter or appendix should be expanded next?
4. Good-first-issue matching thread for new contributors
5. Reader feedback thread for confusing sections and unclear diagrams
6. Tooling thread: mdBook + PDF workflow improvements
7. Monthly progress check-in for learners following the full roadmap
8. Open-source contribution wins inspired by this handbook

## Moderation Guidance

- Keep discussions respectful and technical.
- Redirect reproducible bugs to Issues.
- Move security disclosures to private channels per SECURITY.md.
